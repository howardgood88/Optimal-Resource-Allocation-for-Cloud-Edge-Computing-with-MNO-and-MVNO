The entry of each data file:

* history_data, task_events
    1. index
    1. event_type (start/end)
    1. event_time
    1. task_type
    1. user_id
    1. machine_id
    1. cpu_request
    1. average_cpu_usage
    1. T_up
    1. T_down
    1. from_user (runtime)
    1. avg_bw_up (runtime)
    1. avg_bw_down (runtime)

* machine_attributes
    1. machine_id
        1. task_type
        1. location
        1. cpu_capacity
        1. price
